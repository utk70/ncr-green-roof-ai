The Urban Heat Challenge

Why we need to act now to cool down our cities.

Urban Heat Island (UHI)

Concrete and asphalt absorb solar radiation, making cities 5-10¬∞C hotter than surrounding rural areas. This increases energy demand and health risks.

The Green Roof Solution

Vegetation on roofs cools buildings through evapotranspiration, lowers AC costs by up to 30%, manages stormwater, and cleans the air.

THE GAP

Finding the Critical 1%

Green roofs cost money. Planners can't green every roof. We used AI to identify the Top 1% Critical Buildings that contribute the most heat.

The Algorithm

We processed 385,000+ buildings using Python and Google Earth Engine. Our weighted ranking formula ensures we target large surface areas that are emitting extreme heat.
